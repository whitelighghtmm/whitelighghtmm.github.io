FULL-TIME EMPLOYMENT: Behavior Therapist
In our drive to help children with developmental delays achieve their greatest potential, we at ARN are always on the look-out for like-minded, passionate individuals to join us in changing the lives of these children and their families. Teaching children with autism is a very unique profession. It requires patience, enthusiasm, and commitment.

If you wish to be a professional behavior therapist, we will provide the necessary intensive training on ABA - Verbal Behavior methodology and further professional development. Whether you have an educational background in psychology or other relevant fields of study, come from a different job background, or are fresh out of university, you should possess the following traits:

– fun-loving and energetic
– someone who enjoys working with children
– confident and committed
– patient and empathetic
– a quick and out-of-the-box thinker
– able to work independently and with a team

You could be making a huge difference in helping these children towards a fulfilling and sustainable life. Please email your CV to us at career@autismrecovery.sg. Only shortlisted candidates will be notified.

PART-TIME EMPLOYMENT: Internship

ARN also offers internships for students and supervision for those working towards their BCBA credentials. If you are interested, please email your CV to us at career@autismrecovery.sg. Shortlisted candidates will be notified.
