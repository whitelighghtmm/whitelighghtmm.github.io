Distance / Overseas Therapy

At ARN, we aim to serve the needs of every family, even those living far from the regions currently served by our branches. If you have a pressing need for early intervention and are interested in our highly effective ABA-VB methodology, we’re ready to help.

Our programme is globally sought after. Our consultants have travelled as far as Italy, Spain and Saudi Arabia.

For families who can travel to one of our centres (Singapore, Hong Kong, Indonesia), our services can be customized according to the duration of your trip.

• LEAP Program (short-term, high-intensity ABA/VB)
• International Consultation
• Parent Training
• Speech Therapy and Occupational Therapy
• School Consultation
• Shadow Training

Features

Individualised Education Plan (IEP)

Full-day intensive ABA-VB therapy with an elite team of therapists and supervisors 5.5 days a week at our center during your stay in Singapore.

Consultations with our expert supervisors available at ARN centers, on-site or over Skype.

Available in English, Mandarin, Cantonese, Bahasa-Melayu, and Bahasa-Indonesia.
