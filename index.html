What sets us apart?

We get results

Over 70% of our children enter mainstream schools. Our dedicated therapists are trained directly by Dr. James Partington, Ph.D., BCBA-D; a world-renowned expert on autism and autism treatment. Each of our staff members have 300+ hours of training from the best experts in this field.

We are the most proficient experts of the ABLLS-R

ABLLS-R is a scientifically validated tracking and assessment tool, used to evaluate the basic language and learning skills for children with autism.

We make therapy accessible

We cater to the specific needs of every child. We have a varied list of options you can choose from, allowing you to help your child grow at home and in our centre.

Our Experienced Behavior Analysts

A Board Certified Behavior Analyst® (BCBA®) is a highly trained professional with a graduate-level certification in behavior analysis. At ARN, we work only with the best. Our Behavior Analysts are trained regularly by experts, including the pioneer of ABA-VB therapy, Dr James Partington and are crucial to your child's learning and growth.
