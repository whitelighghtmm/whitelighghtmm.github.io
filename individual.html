1 to 1 ABA Therapy

Our signature program is an advanced form of Applied Behavioral Analysis (ABA) called Verbal Behavior (ABA-VB), which focuses on the acquisition of language skills to help expedite your child’s improvement by equipping and empowering them with the skills to learn more effectively.

Our Advanced Verbal Behaviour Therapy is a progressive and holistic approach to ABA (Applied Behaviour Analysis) treatment. It is an effective and scientifically proven educational process that is steered by your child’s learning needs and goals. Instruction is specifically adapted for your child to maximise learning progress.

When you want to see effective and lasting progress in your child’s development, there is nothing like the power of intensive 1-to-1 ABA therapy. Therapy hours provided may range from 15 hours to up to 40 hours, with the hours needed depending on the specific learning needs and goals of your child.


Who is it for?

• Families with a child diagnosed with special needs and/or developmental delays
• Families who want to see quick acquisition rates
• Families who want to prepare their child for mainstream integration
• Families who have not had success with other early intervention programs
• Families who wish to add ABA to existing early intervention (e.g. EIPIC)
• Families who want maximum results in the shortest time


ARN is proud to be the first in Asia to introduce ABA-VB, known for its highly successful outcomes, especially in language development. We are the only certified ABA-VB provider and the most awarded autism center in Asia. We have been providing effective treatment programs in Asia since 2005.

Every child with autism is different and has unique traits. As such, working with our lead therapists is beneficial because they have worked with many different children and have been exposed to many different cases, including some of the most challenging behaviors brought to ARN over the years. This wide exposure allows them to be more dynamic and creative when addressing problematic behaviors and skill-deficits your child may exhibit.

Our therapy plans are designed for parents looking to start therapeutic intervention immediately. Our clinicians will work around your schedule and create an Individualized Education Plan (IEP) around your child’s needs and availability. The ABLLS-R (Assessment of Basic Language and Learning Skills) is a powerful 3-in-1 diagnostic tool developed by Dr. Partington, that helps us to map out your child’s skills, plan a customized IEP and track our progress over time.

It’s an exciting time when you see your child begin to respond to therapy and acquire new skills. We believe that every step is progress in the right direction and you may choose therapy hours at the frequency you can manage. To see significant improvements in a child’s development, a recommended 15-40 weekly hours of therapy has been proven to maximize the effectiveness of therapy.
