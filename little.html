Little Achievers Preschool

Little Achievers is our Early Intervention Preschool Program that combines early intervention together with an educational curriculum geared towards preparing your child for mainstream schooling.

While children with special needs can benefit from attending an inclusive mainstream preschool program, an Early Intervention Preschool offers a more specialized learning environment that is tailored to help your child learn effectively, overcome challenges and socialize in a group.

Our curriculum approach is informed by over 45 years of clinical experience working with children with developmental needs and we are guided by a pioneer in the field of Early Intervention, Dr. James Partington.


Help Your Child Start School With Confidence

What makes Little Achievers different from other school preparation programs is that our curriculum is not just an academic preschool program to prepare your child for Primary One, but one that is based on scientific research on the order in which children actually learn and acquire skills before starting school.

Based on what we’ve learnt from research, we’ve designed a holistic preschool program to nurture whole child development in 25 areas, including these foundational language and learner skills shown below.
