Social Thinking® Program

At ARN, we use the award-winning Social Thinking® programme created by Michelle Garcia Winner, which goes beyond the teaching of social skills to show children how the social mind works, and how social behaviour influences the way people perceive them.

We are the only ABA service provider in Asia directly trained by Michelle Garcia’s Social Thinking team.

Recognize verbal and nonverbal communication cues

Understand how and why they react and respond in certain ways

Consider the points of view, emotions, beliefs and intentions of others

Ideal for ages 6 up to 13+, 2.5 hours once a week, Early, intermediate and advanced groups available

Our Services

Here at ARN, we also offer a range of services that complement our ABA-VB programmes.

Speech Therapy

In addition to intensive 1-to-1 ABA-VB therapy, many children also benefit from having speech therapy. Communication and language acquisition often form some of the key challenges when it comes with autism and developmental delays.

A speech therapist can complement ABA-VB well in addressing specific language acquisition difficulties such as oral-motor disorders, articulation and fluency.

Read More

Occupational Therapy

Children on the ASD spectrum can benefit tremendously from having Occupational Therapy (OT). At a fundamental level, OT helps children who have difficulty with focus, sensory integration, motor planning and self-regulation.

An occupational therapist is able to help children improve these areas through tasks that can involve music, movement and sensory stimulation. Other areas that can be addressed through OT include working memory skills, visual perception, emotional management and handwriting.

Read More

School Integration Support

Helping your child succeed in school is our top priority. We consult with a mainstream school’s administrative staff, assisting them to accomodate your child’s needs and ensure a smooth transition to their care.

Our school shadow therapist will help your child integrate into a school environment, both inside and outside the classroom. School shadowing can reduce the anxiety and disorientation your child experiences and improve the likelihood of your child adapting well to school.

Our therapists will also provide tutoring and support, even while carrying out your child’s IEP, helping him/her to manage their stress so that they can achieve success in school.
